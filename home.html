About Me

Hello there, I’m Marilyn Muñoz and welcome! I am a hairdresser in Nashville, Tennessee and currently work at Parlour 3 in Brentwood. I was born and raised in West Tennessee but the Nashville atmosphere captured my heart and it is where I hope to grow my career!

My mission in this industry is to remind people how beautiful they are. It is unbelievable how easily someone’s day can be turned around simply by enhancing their natural beauty. I do believe true beauty comes from within BUT there are also a few things that a good hair and makeup day cannot fix! Feel free to take a look at some of my work and send any questions you have my way!
