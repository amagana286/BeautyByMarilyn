About Me

Hello there! I’m Marilyn Muñoz, a recent graduate of the Aveda Institute in Nashville, TN. I was born and raised in a small town known as Dyersburg, TN; but I spent most of my summers and school breaks in a small town in Mexico. After graduating high school, I attended the University of Tennessee at Martin to pursue the path of becoming a dental hygienist. Little did I know, my plans would change as my love for the beauty industry kept growing and now, here I am!

My Mission

My mission in this industry is to remind people how beautiful they are. It is unbelievable how easy someone’s day can be turned around simply by enhancing their natural beauty. There is no better feeling than finishing a service and seeing how happy the guest is. I do believe true beauty comes from within BUT there are also a few things that a good hair day cannot fix!
